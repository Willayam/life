## Content Vision

**The vibe:** Relatable guy who builds cool things with AI, lives a great life in Stockholm, and shares the journey. Not a tech bro - a real person making technology work for real life.

**Content themes:**
1. **Building in public** - Maxa development, app launches, what works and what doesn't
2. **AI for real people** - Teaching non-technical people to use AI (the dad/girlfriend series)
3. **Product thinking** - How to think about what to build and why
4. **Life design** - The journey from consulting to freedom (honest, not guru-ish)
5. **Stockholm life** - Beautiful apartment, friends, family, Scandinavian lifestyle

**Tone:** Genuine, no-hype, smart but accessible, occasionally funny, never preachy.
